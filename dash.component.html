Our motive

The idea behind developing the Kashmir Trails web application is to raise awareness about hiking, trekking and create a database of trails that could be used by other enthusiasts. As we know Kashmir valley being a tourist destination and with the availability of numerous treks (Explored and unexplored) attract a huge number of local and foreign trekkers. No matter if a trekker is local or foreign, he/she needs a trek guide who can guide us on the various trails. But the problem with hiring a guide is that guides ask for a very high amount for guiding on some trail. Being a trekker myself, I have faced a lot of problems in searching the best trail for a trek and moreover being a student my budget couldn’t allow me to hire a guide. So I thought of coming up with a solution that could help the trekkers and help them save a huge chunk of their savings which they are going to spend on hiring a guide. In this modern world where every person is bound to gadgets, everyone needs to take a break and the best way to refresh one’s mind is to go on some trek. So, we wanted to create a platform where the trekkers could get the information about the trek and choose which trail they are taking, while they are planning to go on some trek. The user could visit on our application search about the trek. This web application will benefit both local and foreign trekkers who want to trek in Kashmir.>
